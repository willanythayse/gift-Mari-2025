🌙 The Journey of the Dark Moon ✨

A special adventure for a very special person

🌙

Chapter 1: The Awakening

It was a starry night when Luna, a young witch with curly hair and green eyes, discovered an ancient scroll in her gothic library...

The scroll spoke of a treasure hidden in the depths of the Dark Forest - a magical voucher that granted special wishes.

But to find it, Luna would need to solve three riddles left by the ancient guardians...

🌙

Chapter 2: The First Riddle

Luna arrived at the entrance of the Dark Forest, where a stone statue of a shadowy cat awaited her...

The statue whispered: "To proceed, answer: What is the name of the protagonist of the anime that tells the story of a young man who becomes a shinigami?"

🤔 Riddle 1: This anime is famous for its epic battles and memorable characters. The protagonist has orange hair and fights against hollows.

Attempts remaining: 3

🌙

Chapter 3: The Second Riddle

After solving the first riddle, Luna found a cave illuminated by purple crystals...

Inside the cave, a voice echoed: "Well done, young witch. Now answer: What is the name of the anime where the main characters are 'hunters' who search for treasure?"

🤔 Riddle 2: This anime is known for its Nen system and for having one of the best tournament arcs in anime history.

Attempts remaining: 3

🌙

Chapter 4: The Final Riddle

Luna reached the heart of the Dark Forest, where an ancient tree awaited...

The tree whispered: "Final riddle, young witch. What is the name of the protagonist of the anime where a young ninja dreams of becoming the Hokage of his village?"

🤔 Riddle 3: This anime is famous for its epic ninja battles and for having a protagonist who carries a nine-tailed fox within him.

Attempts remaining: 3

🌙

Final Chapter: The Treasure Revealed

✨ Congratulations! Luna managed to solve all the riddles! ✨

Inside the chest, Luna found a special scroll with a message:
